Predict the Bracket

Call every group, pick the eight best third-place teams, then click your way through the knockouts to crown a champion. Your picks save automatically — share the link to compare.

1 Predict the groups

Tap teams in your predicted finishing order (1st → 4th). Tap a ranked team to remove it.

2 Pick the best thirds 0 / 8

Eight of the twelve third-place teams advance. Choose the eight you think make it.

3 Fill the bracket

Tap the team you think wins each tie to send them through. Round-of-32 matchups use a standard bracket layout for prediction purposes.

Finish predicting all 12 groups to unlock the best-thirds picker and the bracket.
